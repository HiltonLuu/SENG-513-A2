Tutorial

Game Objective

Master Strategist is a fast-paced 2-player game where both players take on the role of a strategists battling against each other. You must guide your troops to victory by tactfully defeating the enemy team.

This can be done through various win conditions.

  1. Defeat 10 enemy units
  2. Reduce the opposing strategist's health to 0

Rules

  • Each strategist begins with 100 health.
  • Every round, both players are given 2 action points that can accumulate between rounds if not used.
  • The battlefield consists of a 2x5 grid with each strategist controlling 1x5 of the board.
  • During their turn, players can do the following actions which should be done in secret of the other player
    • Place a new unit (1 action point): Players can spend 1 action point to place a unit on an open space of their board. They must evaluate the board state to see which unit will be the most effective in the given situation! As long as you have action points and you have space on the board, you can place more units.
    • Swap position (free): Players may freely swap the positioning of units during their turn strategically to gain an advantage over their opposition.
    • End Turn (free): Once a player is satisfied with their actions, they can end their turn, player1 passes to player2. Once player2 ends their turn, combat for the round commences!

Battle

Once both players end their turns, combat commences

  • Starting from left to right, units battle the opposition directly above or below them.
  • If a unit is attacking an empty space, deal damage to the strategist equal to the unit’s physical/magic damage stat.
  • Special Abilities are applied when units attack.
  • If attacking opposing units
    1. Highest speed attacks first and activates any special abilities.
    2. Then the other unit attacks if it survived the other unit’s attack and activates any special abilities.
    3. Units tied in speed attack each other simultaneously and apply special abilities accordingly.
    4. Damage to units = Health - ((Attacker Physical Damage - Defender Physical Defence) + (Attacker Magic Damage - Defender Magical Defence))
  • Extra damage that is dealt as a result of defeating a unit is dealt to the strategist. Example: A wizard deals 15 magic damage to a 5 health knight, then 10 damage is dealt to the strategist who owns the knight.
